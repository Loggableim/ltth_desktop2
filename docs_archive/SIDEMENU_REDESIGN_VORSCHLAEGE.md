## Vorschläge für ein übersichtlicheres Dashboard-Sidemenu (5 Optionen)

Ziel: mehr Unterkategorien ein-/ausklappbar machen und die Navigation logisch clustern, ohne bestehende Funktionen zu entfernen. Alle Optionen lassen sich mit dem bestehenden Kategorie-Collapse-Verhalten umsetzen.

### Option 1: Streaming-Phasen
- **Live-Start & Monitoring**: Dashboard, Events, LastEvent, ClarityHUD, Advanced Timer.
- **Automationen & Abläufe**: Flows, Goals, Automations (Events), Spotlight.
- **Effekte & Audio**: TTS, Talking Heads, Soundboard.
- **Visuals & Overlays**: Emoji Rain, WebGPU Emoji Rain, Fireworks, Vulkan Rockets, Flame Overlay.
- **Admin & Wissen**: Wiki, Settings, Plugin Manager.

### Option 2: Medientypen & Interaktionen
- **Audio & Sprache**: TTS, Soundboard, Talking Heads.
- **Visual FX**: Emoji Rain (beide Varianten), Fireworks (beide Varianten), Flame Overlay.
- **Games & Engagement**: Quiz Show, CoinBattle, Viewer XP, Leaderboard, Gift Milestone.
- **Automationen & Ziele**: Events, Flows, Goals, Advanced Timer, LastEvent/Spotlight.
- **Integrationen**: OSC-Bridge, OpenShock, VDO.Ninja/Multi-Cam, Minecraft Connect, StreamAlchemy, Thermal Printer.
- **Verwaltung**: Dashboard, Wiki, Settings, Plugin Manager.

### Option 3: Use-Cases / Aufgaben
- **Setup & Kontrolle**: Dashboard, Events, ClarityHUD, Advanced Timer.
- **Alerting & Reaktionen**: TTS, Talking Heads, Soundboard, Spotlight.
- **Show-Effekte**: Emoji Rain, WebGPU Emoji Rain, Fireworks, Vulkan Rockets, Flame Overlay.
- **Community-Spiele**: Quiz Show, CoinBattle, Viewer XP, Leaderboard, Gift Milestone.
- **Integrations-Hub**: OSC-Bridge, OpenShock, VDO.Ninja, Minecraft Connect, StreamAlchemy, Thermal Printer.
- **Verwaltung**: Goals, Flows, Wiki, Settings, Plugin Manager.

### Option 4: Kern vs. Erweiterungen
- **Kernfunktionen**: Dashboard, Events, Flows, Goals, LastEvent/Spotlight, ClarityHUD, Advanced Timer.
- **Interaktive Effekte**: TTS, Talking Heads, Soundboard, Emoji Rain (beide), Fireworks (beide), Flame Overlay.
- **Plugins & Tools (erweiterbar)**: OSC-Bridge, OpenShock, VDO.Ninja/Multi-Cam, Quiz Show, Interactive Story, Weather Control, Minecraft Connect, StreamAlchemy, Thermal Printer, Config Import.
- **Experimente & Beta**: GCCE + HUD, Viewer XP, Gift Milestone, CoinBattle.
- **System**: Wiki, Settings, Plugin Manager.

### Option 5: Geräte/Verbindungen zuerst
- **On-Stream Steuerung**: Dashboard, Events, Flows, Goals, Advanced Timer.
- **Audio & Voice**: TTS, Talking Heads, Soundboard.
- **Grafik & Overlay**: Emoji Rain (beide), Fireworks (beide), Flame Overlay, ClarityHUD, LastEvent/Spotlight.
- **Spiele & Engagement**: Quiz Show, CoinBattle, Viewer XP, Leaderboard, Gift Milestone.
- **Hardware/Verbindungen**: OSC-Bridge, OpenShock, VDO.Ninja/Multi-Cam, Minecraft Connect, StreamAlchemy, Thermal Printer, Config Import.
- **Support & Verwaltung**: Wiki, Settings, Plugin Manager.
